# Findings

## Finding 1: Typosquatting

The domain micr0soft-support.com mimics Microsoft by replacing "o" with "0".
This is a known phishing technique used to deceive users.

---

## Finding 2: Social Engineering Pattern

The sender name "security-alert" creates urgency and trust.

This aligns with common phishing tactics:
- account alerts
- password resets
- suspicious login notifications

---

## Finding 3: Elevated Risk Without Reputation Hits

Even if OSINT tools do not show confirmed malicious activity, the domain structure itself is a strong risk indicator.
New phishing domains often have limited reputation data.

---

## Assessment

The domain is suspicious and likely intended for phishing or credential harvesting.

---

## Recommended Actions

- Block or flag the domain
- Investigate email headers
- Search for similar emails
- Alert users
